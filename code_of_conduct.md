# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone contributing to Groot AI.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Communicate constructively
- Support fellow contributors
- Respect different opinions and ideas

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive comments or personal attacks
- Spam or disruptive behavior
- Sharing harmful or inappropriate content

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Contact

For concerns or questions, contact the project maintainers.
